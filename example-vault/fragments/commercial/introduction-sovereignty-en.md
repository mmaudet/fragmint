# Digital Sovereignty — Introduction

As European dependence on US and Chinese technology providers grows, digital sovereignty has become a strategic imperative for public-sector organisations. Recent regulatory developments — including the US Cloud Act, the GDPR, and the NIS2 directive — highlight the urgency of deploying auditable, sovereign-controlled alternatives that can run in fully managed environments.
